Fun Facts About Kittens

  • Kittens sleep 16-20 hours a day to support their rapid growth.
  • They have 26 baby teeth that fall out later for adult teeth.
  • Kittens communicate by meowing, purring, and body language.
  • They begin to open their eyes at around 7 to 10 days old.
  • Kittens can jump up to 5 times their own height!
