Kitten Care Tips

Proper care is essential to keeping your kitten healthy and happy. Here are some tips to help you provide the best care for your furry friend.

Nutrition

Feed your kitten a well-balanced diet formulated specifically for kittens. Fresh water should always be available.

Health

Schedule regular veterinary checkups and vaccinations. Watch for any signs of illness or distress.

Grooming

Brush your kitten regularly to reduce shedding and prevent hairballs, especially for long-haired breeds.

Play & Exercise

Provide plenty of toys and playtime to keep your kitten active and mentally stimulated.

Safety

Keep your home safe by removing toxic plants, securing windows, and keeping small objects out of reach.
