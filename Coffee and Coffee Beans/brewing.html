Brewing Methods

Explore popular coffee brewing methods including espresso, pour-over, French press, and cold brew. Each method highlights different flavors and textures in your cup.

  • Espresso: A concentrated coffee brewed by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee under pressure.
  • Pour-Over: A manual brewing method where hot water is poured over coffee grounds placed in a filter.
  • French Press: Steeping coarsely ground coffee in hot water before pressing with a plunger.
  • Cold Brew: Coffee brewed with cold water over an extended period for a smooth, less acidic flavor.
