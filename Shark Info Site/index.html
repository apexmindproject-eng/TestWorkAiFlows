Shark Habitat

Sharks can be found in oceans all around the world, from coastal shallow waters to deep sea regions. Each species adapts uniquely to their environment.

Interesting Shark Facts

  • Sharks have existed for over 400 million years.
  • They have multiple rows of teeth that are constantly replaced.
  • Sharks detect prey through their acute sense of smell.
  • Some sharks can swim at speeds up to 35 mph.
  • Not all sharks need to keep moving to breathe.
