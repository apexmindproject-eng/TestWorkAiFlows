About Xodus Studios

At Xodus Studios, we are passionate about creating captivating anime entertainment and AAA video games that immerse our audience in unique worlds and stories. Founded by industry veterans with a love for both anime and gaming, our studio combines cutting-edge technology with artistic craftsmanship.

Our Mission

To innovate and deliver high-quality, immersive anime and gaming experiences that captivate and inspire players and fans worldwide. We strive for excellence through creativity, technical proficiency, and a deep understanding of our audience.

Our Team

Our diverse and dedicated team of artists, designers, writers, and developers work collaboratively to bring our projects to life, blending traditional anime aesthetics with modern AAA gaming standards.

    Leading vision and creative direction for all projects.

  • Mia Chen - Lead Animator

    Responsible for character animation and visual storytelling.

  • Jin Seo - Lead Game Developer

    Oversees game mechanics and technical development.
