Feeding & Nutrition

Proper nutrition is the foundation of your kitten's health

Age-Appropriate Food

Kittens under 4 weeks should nurse from their mother or receive kitten milk replacer. From 4-6 weeks, introduce wet kitten food mixed with milk replacer. After 6 weeks, gradually transition to high-quality kitten food formulated for growth.

  • 0-4 weeks: Mother's milk or kitten milk replacer
  • 4-6 weeks: Wet kitten food mixed with milk replacer
  • 6+ weeks: High-quality kitten food
  • 12+ months: Transition to adult cat food

Feeding Schedule

Kittens have small stomachs but high energy needs. Feed them multiple small meals throughout the day to support their rapid growth and development.

  • 6-12 weeks: 4-5 small meals per day
  • 3-6 months: 3-4 meals per day
  • 6-12 months: 2-3 meals per day
  • Always provide fresh water
