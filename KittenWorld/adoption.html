Our Adoption Process

We make sure every kitten finds the perfect home

1

Browse Available Kittens

Explore our available kittens and learn about their personalities, breeds, and special needs. Find the one that matches your lifestyle and preferences.

2

Submit Application

Fill out our detailed adoption application with information about your living situation, experience with pets, and what you're looking for in a kitten.

3

Meet & Greet

Schedule a visit to meet your potential new family member. This helps ensure you and the kitten are a perfect match for each other.

4

Home Visit

Our adoption counselor will conduct a brief home visit to ensure your living space is safe and prepared for a new kitten.

5

Finalize Adoption

Complete the adoption paperwork, pay the adoption fee, and welcome your new furry family member home!
