Comprehensive Recycling Guides

Learn how to recycle properly and make a positive impact on our planet.

Guide to Recycling Plastics

Understand the different types of plastics and how to recycle each one effectively.

  • Identify recyclable plastics by their resin identification codes
  • Rinse and clean plastic containers before recycling
  • Avoid plastic bags and film in curbside bins

Recycling Paper and Cardboard

Proper handling of paper products to ensure they are recycled efficiently.

  • Flatten cardboard boxes to save space
  • Remove non-paper packaging materials
  • Keep paper dry and clean

Metal Recycling Tips

Learn to recycle aluminum cans, tin, and other metals with these simple steps.

  • Rinse and empty metal containers
  • Do not crush cans to facilitate sorting
  • Separate scrap metal from household recyclables

Glass Recycling Guidelines

Follow these guidelines to recycle glass safely and effectively.

  • Sort glass by color if required by your local program
  • Do not recycle broken glass or mirrors in curbside bins
  • Rinse glass containers before recycling
