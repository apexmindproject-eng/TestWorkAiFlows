Baking Tips for Perfect Cookies

Preheat Your Oven Properly

Make sure to preheat your oven to the precise temperature required before baking your cookies. This ensures even baking and perfect texture.

Measure Ingredients Accurately

Using the right measurements, especially for flour and sugar, is crucial. Too much flour can make cookies dry; too little can make them spread too much.

Use Quality Ingredients

Opt for fresh butter, high-quality vanilla extract, and fresh baking soda or powder for the best taste and texture.

Chill Your Dough

Chilling the dough before baking helps control spreading and creates thicker, chewier cookies.

Watch Baking Time Carefully

Check your cookies a few minutes before the recommended baking time. Every oven is different, so visual cues like golden edges are key.
