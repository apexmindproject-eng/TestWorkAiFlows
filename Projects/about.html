About United Self Defense Academy

The United Self Defense Academy (USDA) proudly continues the tradition of Kenpo Karate instruction and training, having evolved independently from the USSD Kenpo Karate lineage. Our programs are designed for students of all ages and skill levels, emphasizing practical self-defense techniques, discipline, and community.

Our academy is dedicated to fostering martial arts growth, preserving our historical roots, and empowering students through personalized instruction and a supportive environment. Whether you are a beginner or an advanced martial artist, USDA offers a comprehensive curriculum to suit your needs.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality Kenpo Karate education that builds confidence, skill, and respect in a safe and motivating atmosphere.

Our Values

  • Discipline and Respect
  • Continuous Learning and Improvement
  • Community and Support
  • Integrity and Honor
  • Personal Empowerment
