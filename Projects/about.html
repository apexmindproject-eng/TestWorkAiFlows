About Zombie Media Encyclopedia

This website serves as an informational resource styled like Wikipedia, dedicated to exploring zombies in television media. Targeted at horror enthusiasts, TV buffs, and pop culture researchers, it documents notable zombie characters, appearances, and the evolution of their roles in real TV shows across decades.

The site features structured articles, searchable content, categorized pages, and references to episodes and shows, making it a comprehensive resource for anyone interested in the zombie genre on television.
