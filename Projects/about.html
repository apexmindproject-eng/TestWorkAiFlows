About USDA Karate

Discover our rich history and commitment to traditional Kenpo Karate

Our Mission

At USDA Karate, we are dedicated to preserving and teaching the traditional art of Kenpo Karate. Our mission is to provide students with authentic martial arts training that builds character, discipline, and physical fitness while honoring the rich heritage of this ancient art.

Our History

Founded with a deep respect for traditional martial arts, USDA Karate has been serving the martial arts community for years. Our dojo represents a branch of authentic Kenpo Karate, maintaining the traditional values and techniques passed down through generations of masters.

What We Offer

  • Traditional Kenpo Karate training
  • Experienced and certified instructors
  • Quality martial arts equipment and gear
  • Programs for all skill levels
  • Focus on discipline, respect, and personal growth

Our Core Values

Discipline

We instill self-discipline through structured training and traditional martial arts principles.

Respect

Respect for instructors, fellow students, and the art itself is fundamental to our teaching.

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all aspects of training, from technique to character development.

Tradition

We honor the traditional methods and philosophy of authentic Kenpo Karate.
