About EarthCarePortal

EarthCarePortal is an educational website dedicated to promoting recycling and sustainable practices to protect the planet. It targets the general public, students, educators, and community organizers with accessible guides, interactive tools, and local recycling resources.

Key Features

  • Step-by-step recycling guides
  • Interactive recycling quiz
  • Recycling locator for local drop-off points
  • Downloadable educational resources
  • Volunteer and contact forms for community involvement
