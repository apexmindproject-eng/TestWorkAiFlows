Biography of Jeffrey Dahmer

Jeffrey Dahmer was an American serial killer and sex offender who committed the murder and dismemberment of 17 men and boys between 1978 and 1991. His crimes involved necrophilia and cannibalism, making his case one of the most infamous in U.S. criminal history.

Dahmer's early life, criminal history, and psychological background are documented here to provide a factual and researched perspective for students, researchers, educators, and general readers interested in criminal history.

This website presents a respectful and verified account of Dahmer's biography, highlighting the importance of remembering the victims.
