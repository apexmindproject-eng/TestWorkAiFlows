Kitten Care Tips

Helpful advice for new and experienced kitten parents to ensure a happy, healthy cat.

Feeding Your Kitten

Provide a balanced diet formulated specifically for kittens. Frequent small meals help support their growth and energy needs.

Health and Vet Visits

Schedule regular veterinary checkups for vaccinations and health screening to keep your kitten safe and thriving.

Safe and Enriching Environment

Create a kitten-proof home with plenty of toys, scratching posts, and cozy resting spots to promote play and rest.

Socialization and Training

Gently expose your kitten to new people, sounds, and experiences to raise a confident and well-adjusted cat.

Grooming and Hygiene

Regular brushing, nail trims, and dental care help keep your kitten comfortable and looking their best.
