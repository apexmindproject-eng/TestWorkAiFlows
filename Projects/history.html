History of Cookies

Discover the rich and delicious history behind the world of cookies, from ancient origins to modern favorites.

The Origin of Cookies

Cookies are believed to have originated in 7th century Persia, shortly after the use of sugar became common in the region. The word cookie is derived from the Dutch word "koekje," meaning little cake.

Evolution Through the Ages

As the recipe for cookies traveled across Europe and eventually to America, it evolved into many different forms and flavors, reflecting the ingredients and traditions of each culture.

Modern Cookies and Popular Varieties

Today, cookies come in an array of flavors and textures, from classic chocolate chip to oatmeal raisin, inspiring bakers and cookie lovers worldwide to create their own unique recipes.
