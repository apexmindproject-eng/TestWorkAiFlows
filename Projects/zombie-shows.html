Zombie Shows

The Walking Dead

A post-apocalyptic horror TV series focused on a group of survivors navigating a world dominated by zombies. It has greatly influenced zombie media and popular culture.

Notable appearances: Rick Grimes, Daryl Dixon, Michonne

Decade: 2010s - 2020s

Z Nation

A more action-oriented and comedic take on zombie apocalypse, it follows a group tasked with transporting a survivor immune to the zombie virus across the country.

Notable appearances: Murphy, Doc, 10K

Decade: 2010s
