The Jeffrey Dahmer Archive

A factual, researched website presenting the history and background of serial killer Jeffrey Dahmer for students, researchers, educators, and general readers interested in criminal history. The site provides an interactive timeline, detailed case summaries, a media gallery, searchable records, and a bibliography of sources while emphasizing respectful presentation of victims and verified citations.
