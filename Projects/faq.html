Frequently Asked Questions

What ingredients do you use in your products?

We use natural, locally sourced ingredients including essential oils, shea butter, beeswax, and organic herbs to ensure quality and safety.

Do you offer gift wrapping?

Yes, we offer eco-friendly gift wrapping options at checkout for an additional small fee.

Can I track my order?

Once your order has shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email to monitor your package's progress.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for unopened products. Please contact our support for assistance.

How can I contact customer service?

You can reach us through our Contact page or email support@artisanskincare.com directly.
