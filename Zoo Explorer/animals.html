Animals of the Zoo

Lion

Known as the king of the jungle, lions are majestic big cats with a powerful roar and distinctive mane.

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals, known for their intelligence, memory, and strong family bonds.

Giraffe

The giraffe is the tallest animal on earth, recognizable by its long neck and unique spotted coat.

Zebra

Zebras are herbivores known for their distinctive black and white stripes which help with camouflage and social connection.
