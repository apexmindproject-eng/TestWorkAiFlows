Classic Glazed Donut

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients together in a bowl.
  2. In a separate bowl, whisk eggs, milk, and melted butter.
  3. Combine wet and dry ingredients until smooth.
  4. Heat oil in a deep fryer or pan to 3506F (1756C).
  5. Fry doughnut dough until golden brown on both sides.
  6. Drain and glaze immediately.

Chocolate Donut

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup buttermilk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 3506F (1756C) and grease donut pans.
  2. Mix dry ingredients in a large bowl.
  3. Whisk buttermilk, eggs, and oil in another bowl.
  4. Combine wet and dry ingredients until smooth.
  5. Fill donut pans and bake for 12-15 minutes.
  6. Cool and frost with chocolate glaze.

Sprinkle Donut

Ingredients

  • Prepared glazed donuts
  • Sprinkles of your choice

Instructions

  1. Dip glazed donuts in melted icing or glaze.
  2. Sprinkle generously with colorful sprinkles.
  3. Let set before serving.
