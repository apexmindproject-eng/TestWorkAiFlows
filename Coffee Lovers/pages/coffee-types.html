Coffee Types

Arabica

Arabica beans are known for their smooth, sweet flavor and higher acidity. They make up the majority of the world's coffee production and are cherished for their delicate aroma and taste.

Robusta

Robusta beans have a stronger, more bitter flavor with a higher caffeine content. They are often used in espresso blends to provide a fuller body and crema.

Liberica

Liberica is a rare coffee type with a unique floral and fruity flavor. It is grown primarily in the Philippines and parts of Malaysia.

Excelsa

Excelsa coffee offers a tart, fruity profile with a dark, mysterious complexity. Once grouped with Liberica, it is now recognized as a distinct species.
