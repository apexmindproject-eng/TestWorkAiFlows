Coffee Varieties

Explore the diverse and fascinating varieties of coffee beans from around the world, each with unique flavors and characteristics.

Arabica

Arabica beans are known for their smooth, complex flavor and aromatic qualities. They account for the majority of the world's coffee production.

Robusta

Robusta beans have a stronger, harsher taste with a grainy or nutty flavor. They contain more caffeine than Arabica beans and are often used in espresso blends.

Liberica

Liberica beans are rare and unique, with a smoky, floral aroma and a slightly woody taste. They are grown mainly in the Philippines and Malaysia.

Excelsa

Excelsa beans are often used to provide a tart, fruity complexity to coffee blends. They grow primarily in Southeast Asia and have a distinctive flavor profile.
