Welcome to Coconut Tree Info

Discover everything about coconut trees, including their characteristics, uses, and importance to ecology and economy.

Characteristics

Coconut trees can grow up to 30 meters tall with slender trunks and a crown of large, feathery leaves.

Uses of Coconut Trees

Coconuts are used for culinary purposes, oil extraction, and various food products.

Leaves are used for thatching roofs, making baskets and mats, and for decoration.

Importance

Coconut trees play a vital role in coastal ecosystems and support the livelihood of millions of people around the world.
